About Adorn by SOC

Jewellery That Lives Your Life With You

Adorn by SOC is an Indian anti-tarnish jewellery brand that makes waterproof, 18K gold-plated jewellery on 316L surgical-grade stainless steel. Designed in Pune. Made for everyday Indian life — the office, the gym, the monsoon.

The Founder
"Luxury should feel personal, wearable, and accessible to everyone."

Why We Started

Hi, I'm Shivani, a fashion designer and personal stylist from Pune. After styling hundreds of people through SOC, I realised that finding jewellery that feels elegant, versatile, and affordable is still difficult — so I created Adorn by SOC, where every piece is personally handpicked to complement real outfits, real occasions, and everyday life.

For me, Adorn is a heartfelt effort to make luxury feel more personal, wearable, and accessible to everyone.

— Shivani
The Honest-Specs Manifesto

We Publish Our Specs. Ask Any Other Brand to Do the Same.

Real gold is a locker purchase. Imitation fades in weeks. We build the first option: pieces on a 316L surgical-grade stainless steel base — the alloy used in medical implants and luxury watches — sealed in 18K gold plating. Steel doesn't oxidise in normal wear, so there's nothing underneath to turn black. That's the whole secret, and we'd rather say it plainly than hide it behind adjectives.
