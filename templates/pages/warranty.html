Our Promise

The 6-Month Anti-Tarnish Warranty

If any Adorn by SOC piece fades, tarnishes, or loses its plating within 6 months of normal wear, we replace it free. No receipts hunt, no interrogation.

How It Works

  • 1. Take a photo of the affected piece.
  • 2. WhatsApp us the photo along with your order ID.
  • 3. We ship a replacement — a new piece dispatches within 48 hours of approval.

What's Covered

  • Fading, tarnishing, or blackening of the plating in normal daily wear.
  • Plating loss within 6 months of your delivery date.

What's Not Covered

  • Physical damage — bent, broken, or crushed pieces.
  • Damage from harsh chemicals: chlorine bleach, acetone, or perfume sprayed directly on the piece.
  • Lost or stolen items.

Why We Can Offer This

Our jewellery is built on 316L surgical-grade stainless steel with 18K gold plating. Steel doesn't oxidise in normal wear — so there's nothing underneath to turn black. We publish our specs because we're confident in them; the warranty is that confidence in writing.
